FAQ

What is this?
These maps are forecasts of when specific things like flowers or leaves will appear for a variety of plant species around the US.
How are they made?
All of these maps are made with climate forecasts and different kinds of growing degree day phenology models.
By who?
This is an extension of research by Shawn Taylor, an ecologist in the White Lab at the University of Florida.
Why?
Ecologists have a lot of opportunities to help others by making forecasts about what we study. This is an experiment down that path.
